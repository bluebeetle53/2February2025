Music Player

How to Use:
  • Step 1: Click on the "Choose Files" button to select audio files from your device.
  • Step 2: After selecting files, they will appear in the list below.
  • Step 3: Click on any file name in the list to play the music.
  • Step 4: Adjust the volume using the volume slider.
  • Step 5: Click the "Stop" button to stop the music and reset.
